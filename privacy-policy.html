Privacy Policy

Effective starting: October 1, 2025

Thank you for choosing Natural Studio (“we,” “our,” “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our software, Natural Studio, and any related online services (collectively, the “Services”).

By using our software, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Natural Studio is an AI-powered screen recording software for Windows PCs. It allows users to record their screen, audio, and webcam, and optionally upload their recordings to cloud storage platforms (like Google Drive) for sharing or backup.

We respect your privacy and ensure that your data is handled responsibly and transparently.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve Natural Studio.

a. Information You Provide

When using Natural Studio or contacting us, you may provide:

• Email address or contact info (if you register, subscribe, or contact support).

• Account information (if Natural Studio includes sign-in or license activation).

• Payment information (if applicable, handled securely by third party payment processors).

• Feedback or support messages.

b. Usage Data

When you use Natural Studio, we may collect limited technical data to improve performance, including:

• Application logs (errors, crashes, performance metrics).

• Device details (Windows version, hardware type).

• IP address (for licensing or activation, if applicable).

c. Recording Data

• All screen recordings, webcam captures, and audio you create are stored locally on your PC by default.

• We do not automatically upload, access, or view your recordings.

• You control whether recordings are shared or uploaded to cloud platforms like Google Drive.

d. Third-Party Integrations

If you connect Google Drive or another cloud service:

• We access only the information required to upload, store, and manage your files.

• We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication — we never store your passwords.

• This integration follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Operate and maintain the app.

• Improve functionality, stability, and performance.

• Respond to your inquiries and provide support.

• Authenticate user licenses (if applicable).

• Comply with security and legal requirements.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. AI Features and Data Processing

Natural Studio uses artificial intelligence (AI) features.

When these features are used:

• Processing happens locally on your PC or securely on our servers (only if the feature requires cloud processing).

• Your recordings are not used to train public AI models.

• We only process data to provide the requested AI feature, then delete or anonymize it immediately after processing.

5. How We Share Information

We may share limited information with:

• Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processors).

• Cloud storage platforms (like Google Drive), only when you authorize uploads.

• Legal authorities, if required by law.

• Business successors, if Natural Studio is acquired or merged.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for legitimate purposes.

6. Data Storage and Security

• Your recordings and local data remain on your Windows device unless you choose to upload them.

• We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your data during transmission (e.g., uploads).

• Regular audits and security practices help prevent unauthorized access or breaches.

However, no digital system is 100% secure, and you should take care to protect your files and credentials.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

• Provide and maintain the Services.

• Resolve technical issues or user requests.

• Comply with legal obligations.

• You may request the deletion of your data at any time (see Section 10).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

• Access, correct, or delete your data.

• Withdraw consent for data processing.

• Request data portability.

• Object to specific data uses.

Contact us at uimultisoft@outlook.com to make a request. We will respond in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children’s Privacy

Natural Studio is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Data Deletion and Account Removal

You can:

• Delete recordings manually from your PC or cloud storage.

• Disconnect Google Drive access at any time.

• Request deletion of your personal data or user account by contacting uimultisoft@outlook.com.

Once your data is deleted, we retain only minimal records necessary for legal or security purposes.

11. GDPR (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Legal Bases for Processing:

• Contractual necessity.

• Consent (for optional features or communications).

• Legitimate interest (e.g., improving functionality).

• Legal obligation.

You have the right to access, correct, erase, or restrict processing of your personal data. You can contact us at uimultisoft@outlook.com

12. CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you are entitled to rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

• Know what data we collect and how we use it.

• Request deletion of your data.

• Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data).

• Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

• submit a request, email us at uimultisoft@outlook.com with the subject line “CCPA Request.”

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed or stored on servers outside your country. We take measures to ensure your data is transferred securely and in compliance with applicable laws (e.g., using EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

14. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our technology or legal obligations. We’ll notify you of material updates by posting a notice in the app or on our website. The “Effective Date” above reflects the latest revision.
